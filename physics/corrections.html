Corrections

Instantons in QCD

The following is an (incomplete?) list of errors and msiprints in "Instantons in QCD", Rev. Mod. Phys. 70, 323 (1998).
Please contact me at thomas@tonic.physics.sunysb.edu if you come across anything in that review that seems wrong or mysterious.
  • page 347, equ. (112). Replace 1.34 -> (1.34)^N_f
  • page 376, equ. (260). Replace p^4 -> p^3.
    page 376, equ. (261). Replace p^3 -> p^2.
  • page 386, equ. (386). Shuryak reports that this equation is not correct.
    Use equ. (4) of (Schaefer and Shuryak 1995a).
  • page 389, above equ. (305). ".. where the fields are very strong, G_\mu\nu \sim \beta^2".
    Replace G_\mu\nu \sim \beta^2 -> G_\mu\nu \sim \beta^-2
  • page 421, equ. (A34). Replace (\lambda^a)_jk (\lambda^a)_li (\lambda^b)_no (\lambda^b)_mp ->
    (\lambda^a)_kj (\lambda^b)_il (\lambda^a)_on (\lambda^b)_mp
