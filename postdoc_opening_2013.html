The Department of Physics at North Carolina State University has an opening for a postdoctoral research associate to begin fall 2013. Applicants must have a Ph.D. in physics, with a background in nuclear physics or particle physics. Applicants with expertise in QCD, the physics of hot and dense matter, hadronic physics, many body physics, and effective field theory are especially encouraged to apply.

Interested candidates should apply by visiting https://jobs.ncsu.edu and designate position number 00100626. Applicants will be required to attach a CV, list of publications, statement of research interests. Please have three letters of recommendation sent to Prof. T. Schaefer (tmschaef@ncsu.edu).

Applications will be reviewed beginning March 1, 2013. NC State University is an Equal Opportunity and Affirmative Action employer. In addition, NC State welcomes all persons without regard to sexual orientation or genetic information. Persons with disabilities requiring accommodations in the application and interview process please call (919) 515-3148.
